After the service is started, it is necessary to provide external services through the Ingress.
The service entry is: http://$$NODE_IP:80
Please confirm whether the Ingress-NGINX component has been installed in kubernetes cluster.
Refer to: https://github.com/kubernetes/ingress-nginx.
Now, you can query the detailed service status by executing 'kubectl get po'

请先确认kubernetes集群是否已安装Ingress-NGINX组件,可参考: https://github.com/kubernetes/ingress-nginx.
现在,可以通过执行`kubectl get po`查询详细服务状态.
